The World's Best Taco Bowls!

One of the best things about the taco bowl recipe is how easily customizable it is! Below is a list of my suggested ingredients, but feel free to mix and match the toppings of your choice.

  • 3 cups of white or brown rice
  • 2-3 lbs of ground beef
  • 70 g of taco seasoning
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, thinly diced
  • 540 mL of canned black beans
  • 260 mL of canned corn
  • 500 mL of salsa
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • Salt, to taste
  • Cayenne pepper, to taste

And the dish is incredibly simple to make! Just follow the following steps:

  1. Begin cooking the rice (ideally, in a rice cooker). Once ready, set aside to cool.
  2. Fry the onions and garlic in a large pot.
  3. Once the onions are translucent, add the ground beef and cook fully. Drain excess liquid.
  4. Add approximately one cup of water and the taco seasoning. Stir until the spice has been absorbed.
  5. Mix in bell peppers, black beans, corn, salsa, salt, and cayenne pepper. Cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  6. Portion mixture into bowls of rice. Add in crushed tortilla chips.

Enjoy! This recipe made me 6 servings. I'd recommend freezing anything you don't plan to eat in the next couple days.

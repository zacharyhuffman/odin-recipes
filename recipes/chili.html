The Perfect Hearty Chili

This chili was actually one of my first meal preps, and during the winter, I still go back to it every few weeks. I've listed my recommended ingredients below:

  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 75 g of chili seasoning
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 2 540 mL cans of red kidney beams
  • 1 540 mL can of black beans
  • 1 420 mL can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 420 mL can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

To make, just follow the following steps:

  1. Combine ground beef and chili seasoning.
  2. In a large pot, brown ground beef, onion, and garlic. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add in remaining ingredients an bring to a boil. Reduce heat and summer uncovered for 45-60 minutes.
  4. Enjoy! Yes, it really was that easy!
